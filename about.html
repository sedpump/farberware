About the Brand

We bring convenience, dependability, and value to America’s busy kitchens. It's a Farberware tradition we trace back to 1900 when S.W. Farber made his first tin and copper cookware. Since then, Farberware has remained committed to making everyday cooking easier for generations of home cooks. From reliable aluminum and stainless clad cookware to the iconic stovetop coffee percolator to the latest in hybrid ceramic nonstick, Farberware continues to bring durability, design, innovation and value that makes it America’s most trusted cookware brand. Made for every generation, Farberware helps today’s families stay one step ahead in the kitchen with quality, convenience, and 120 years of reliability… and counting.
